Personal or Corporate Partnerships and Sponsorships

Stemming from a young friend’s tragic sexual abuse, I wrote a children’s book entitled Tootles the Turtle Tells the Truth. In the book, written to ease the terror associated with abuse and bullying for fear of retribution, a small alligator bites off Tootles leg when she wanders too near the edge of the lake. The gluttonous gator warns Tootles, “Don’t you tell a soul OR ELSE! Not ONE SOUL or I’ll come back and eat your other legs one by one and FINALLY--I’ll have your Mommy for dessert!

Of course Tootles takes the alligator at his word and doesn’t tell a soul about the warning, especially not Mommy, for fear of losing her. When Tootles inadvertently tells her new friend, Freddie the Fearless Free-Spirited Frog, an exaggerated version of what happened to her leg, she becomes physically sick with worry that the alligator will come back to carry out his threats.

Tootles helped my friend’s child tell what happened to her without fear of her mommy “going to jail" if she told anyone. The sad truth is that this happens much too often and children ARE terribly afraid of the threats made by perpetrators and bullies. I am proud to have many wonderful endorsements from respected counselors and therapist who believe that it would truly benefit children of any age. In addition, the end of the book has interactive questions from Tootles and Freddie encouraging candid discussions about good secrets and bad or scary secrets.

Please consider a personal or corporate partnership with Team Tootles Foundation Inc. It is a great way to assist in distributing and placing the timely, enlightening book with schools, libraries, counselors, pediatricians, children’s hospitals, etc. free of charge to benefit as many children as possible.

I am a Reiki Master, certified Tai Chi, Qigong, and Sacred Heart Yoga instructor, certified spiritual healer, life enrichment coach, artist, writer, and most importantly, mother. In addition to over 25 years of experience in the holistic health industry, I have taught workshops and development classes in and around the Baton Rouge and south Louisiana area for the past several years.

My mission in this lifetime is to empower others to live free from emotional as well as physical pain. My heartfelt belief is that the future of our children must be protected at all costs. I truly feel that if this book can prevent, at the very least, one child from experiencing the horror of sexual abuse or bullying it will be well worth the effort.

Thank you for considering a partnership with me and Tootles for this Divinely-inspired project of love.

Lenell
