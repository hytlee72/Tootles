Mission Statement

Team Tootles Foundation, Inc is a group of like-minded individuals joining hearts to empower children with knowledge through enlightening, timely books and supporting materials encouraging them to live in joy and freedom from emotional, as well as physical, pain or harm.

Our goal is to empower children by disseminating the meaningful messages in these books and materials to benefit as many children as possible through distribution to counselors, pediatricians, schools, libraries, children’s hospitals, etc. free of charge.

Our Purpose

A. Publish, promote, and disseminate empowering books and instructional materials to foster public awareness of children’s emotional and physical health and safety issues.

B. To educate children so as to empower them to protect themselves from abuse, neglect or exploitation.

C. To encourage children to speak out on perceived violations of their personal boundaries.

D. To instill in children a solid sense of self-worth and the sanctity of their bodies, personal space, and boundaries.

E. To provide instructional materials to professionals to assist with recognizing and counseling children’s issues

F. To provide libraries, pediatricians, schools, hospitals, clinics, daycare centers, other charitable organizations concerned with children’s issues, etc. with these empowering instructional materials free of charge.
